SMS & MMS Messaging Opt-In Policy

By providing your mobile number and opting in, you consent to receive SMS and MMS messages from Axis Texas, including updates, special offers, and important notifications.

How to Opt In

You can opt in to receive messages by:

  • Filling out a form on our website
  • Checking a box during account registration or checkout
  • Texting a keyword like JOIN to our short code or number
  • Providing verbal consent during a phone call with our representative

Message frequency: No more than four messages per month. Message and data rates may apply.

How to Opt Out

You can cancel at any time by texting STOP to our number. After doing so, you will no longer receive messages from us. You may receive a final confirmation message.
